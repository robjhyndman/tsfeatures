# tsfeatures: Time Series Feature Extraction

Methods for extracting various features from time series data. The
features provided are those from Hyndman, Wang and Laptev (2013)
[doi:10.1109/ICDMW.2015.104](https://doi.org/10.1109/ICDMW.2015.104) ,
Kang, Hyndman and Smith-Miles (2017)
[doi:10.1016/j.ijforecast.2016.09.004](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijforecast.2016.09.004)
and from Fulcher, Little and Jones (2013)
[doi:10.1098/rsif.2013.0048](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2013.0048) .
Features include spectral entropy, autocorrelations, measures of the
strength of seasonality and trend, and so on. Users can also define
their own feature functions.
